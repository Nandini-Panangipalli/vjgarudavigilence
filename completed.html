Academic Events

Internal CTF


We regularly host Capture The Flag (CTF) events, offering students hands-on opportunities to test their cybersecurity skills. Internal CTF competitions limited to our college are held once Every Month for 1-2 hours, fostering continuous skill development. These events serve as invaluable platforms for honing practical cybersecurity techniques and simulating real-world scenarios to enhance problem-solving abilities

External CTF


We organize External Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions once per semester, attracting participants from various institutions. Our role as organizers allows us to curate engaging challenges and provide a platform for showcasing cybersecurity skills. These events promote networking and collaboration within the cybersecurity community while enhancing participants' problem-solving abilities
