What is this
club about?

Garuda Vigilance Club is a Cyber Security Club. This club aims to expertise both the faculty and students in this alarming Cyber World.

Objective

  1. Practical Demonstration & Hands On Experience
  2. Knowledge Sharing Lecture Series (KSLS)
  3. Creating Awareness
  4. Certification and so on...

Aim

  1. Develop a Knowledge Hub in Cyber Security.
  2. Establishment of National Level Center of Excellence in Cyber Security
  3. Partnership for Startups.
  4. Designing SOPs for Cyber Security.

Events Conducted

  1. KSLS – 1 (Introduction to Ethical Hacking and Footprinting)
  2. KSLS – 2 (Security Incident Response – A Practical Approach)
  3. KSLS – 3 (Tools for Passive Footprinting, Zenmap Tool)
  4. KSLS – 4 (SQL Injection – A Practical Approach)
  5. University level Competition on CyberSecurity & Narcotic Drugs
  6. Ethical Hacking YouTube Series by Dr. M. Rajashekar
